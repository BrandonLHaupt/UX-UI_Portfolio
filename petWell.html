Overview

I participated in a Hackathon by General Assembly to help pet owners be reminded of vaccinations and medications for their pets

Roles

UI Designer Interaction design, Prototyping, Wireframing, Layout Design, Design Systems

Goals

Our goal was to make it easier for pet owners be reminded when their next vet appointment would be and when they need to give their pet their medicine

Background

I was the UI designer on the team along with a UX Researcher, UX Generalist, and four engineers. I was responsible for the product design and the UI experience for the app.

This hackathon was a week long, my team and I tackled the issue of pet owners forgetting when to give their pets medicine or when to take them for their next vaccine appointment. We started to research how many pet owners have forgotten to give their pets medicine, what the symptoms were, or when their last vaccine was taken. From this research, we have discovered that it was a lot more than expected. Out of the 31 responses, 45.2% were not sure when their pet's vaccines were due and 64.5% of respondents did not remember any symptoms their pet had when taking medicine.
